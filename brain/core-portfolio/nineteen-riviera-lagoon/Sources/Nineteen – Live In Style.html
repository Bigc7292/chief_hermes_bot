*  A City Within the City

Dubai's position as the preeminent destination in the Gulf is assured, attracting visitors and residents from across the world.

Any introduction to this nexus of sophistication and style is unnecessary, as Dubai continually garners headlines for its vibrancy, opulence and dynamism. Within Dubai, Mohammed Bin Rashid City stands apart. This exclusive enclave has grown into an exceptional cultural, business and commercial hub with its residential core in District 11. It is here that Nineteen Riviera Lagoon has blossomed. This development is unlike any other in this district, distinctive for its uniquely appointed villas and the tranquil location in proximity to Dubai's centre.
